## What Happened

Investigative reporting revealed that LSC had been systematically overcharging rim systems for lattice drive maintenance while undercharging core systems. The pricing disparity exacerbated the core-rim economic divide. Director Meridian defended the pricing as "market-responsive."

## Consequences & Aftermath

GTA investigation launched (ongoing, unlikely to produce meaningful results). Increased scrutiny of LSC's practices. The scandal provided context for Keth'vol's later discovery of deeper financial anomalies.
