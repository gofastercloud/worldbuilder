## What Happened

Human explorers encountered a Keth'ri mining expedition in what would become the Crossroads System. Communication difficulties and mutual suspicion led to a brief armed confrontation — the first inter-species military conflict in recorded history.

## Consequences & Aftermath

Two brief wars over the following century before economic interdependence made peace more profitable than conflict. The experience shaped both species' approach to inter-species relations — humans learned to negotiate before shooting, and the Keth'ri learned that not all species think collectively.
