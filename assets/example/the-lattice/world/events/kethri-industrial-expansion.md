## What Happened

Following spaceflight achievement, the Keth'ri Collective systematically expanded their industrial base across their home system and nearby stars. The Great Shipyards began construction. The manufacturing infrastructure that would later serve the entire galaxy took shape.

## Consequences & Aftermath

By the time other species encountered them, the Keth'ri had the most advanced manufacturing capability in the galaxy — a position they have maintained ever since.
