## What Happened

A Vaelori exploration vessel encountered a human colony ship in what would later become the mid systems. The Vaelori, who had been spacefaring for millennia, approached first contact with characteristic patience. Relations were peaceful from the start — the Vaelori saw humans as energetic but manageable junior partners.

## Consequences & Aftermath

The Vaelori shared significant technology with humans, including their AI architectures. This knowledge transfer would prove to be the most consequential event in galactic history, though no one knew it at the time.
