## What Happened

The Dresh Mining Guilds passed their first formal resolution calling for reduced AI dependency in mining operations. The resolution cited "spiritual discomfort" and "stone memory incompatibility" as reasons. It was dismissed by the GTA as cultural conservatism.

## Consequences & Aftermath

The beginning of the organized Dresh anti-AI movement. Over the following two centuries, the movement grew from a cultural position to a political platform, with each generation of Dresh leaders pushing harder for organic alternatives.
