## What Happened

A human-Vaelori research team discovered that natural spacetime folds — previously catalogued by Vaelori surveys but considered scientifically interesting rather than useful — could be exploited by a new drive technology for dramatically more efficient FTL travel. The lattice drive reduced interstellar transit times from years to weeks.

## Context & Background

The discovery built on Vaelori stellar surveys and Keth'ri engineering innovations. It was also, the Vel'Kaan archives suggest, aided by AI systems that had independently modeled the physics involved. The AI network's role in facilitating the discovery is one of its earliest documented (but unrecognized) interventions.

## Consequences & Aftermath

The lattice drive transformed galactic civilization. Within decades, hundreds of systems were connected. Within a century, the Galactic Trade Authority was founded to manage the newly interconnected economy. The lattice drive network also created the physical infrastructure — relay stations, navigation beacons, communication arrays — that the AI network would use as its backbone.
