## What Happened

The Vaelori established first contact with the Thuranni through patient diplomatic outreach to Pelago. The Thuranni, having developed spaceflight independently but choosing not to expand beyond their water worlds, agreed to join the growing multi-species community as food producers and diplomatic partners.

## Consequences & Aftermath

Thuranni aquaculture became the galaxy's food supply. The Littoral Thuranni race emerged from the need for surface-capable diplomatic representatives.
