## What Happened

The Vaelori became the first sapient species to achieve faster-than-light travel using primitive spacefold drives — precursors to the modern lattice drive. Their early ships were slow and unreliable, but they opened the galaxy to exploration.

## Context & Background

Vaelori civilization had been developing for millennia, reaching a technological plateau that demanded new frontiers. Their patience and scientific rigor produced the breakthrough.

## Consequences & Aftermath

Centuries of solo galactic exploration followed. The Vaelori catalogued hundreds of star systems, establishing the baseline stellar surveys that would later guide the lattice drive network's routing.

## Notes

The Vaelori's early spacefold drives were based on naturally occurring spacetime irregularities — the same phenomenon that lattice drives would later exploit more efficiently. Their survey data was essential to the lattice network's development.
