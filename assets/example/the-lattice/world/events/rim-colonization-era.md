## What Happened

The lattice drive network's expansion opened hundreds of new systems for colonization. Corporate-funded settlement programs promised opportunity and delivered resource extraction. Rim worlds were settled primarily by humans and Dresh, with Mek'vol generation ships serving as supply chains.

## Consequences & Aftermath

The rim became economically productive but politically powerless. The contracts governing resource extraction systematically favored core corporations. The seeds of the core-rim divide were planted during this period.
