## What Happened

The Keth'ri Collective achieved spaceflight through a characteristically methodical approach — no heroic solo missions, just collective engineering solving problems until the problems were solved. Their early ships were robust and practical.

## Consequences & Aftermath

The Keth'ri expanded into nearby systems for industrial resources, establishing the manufacturing base that would later make them the galaxy's dominant shipbuilders.
