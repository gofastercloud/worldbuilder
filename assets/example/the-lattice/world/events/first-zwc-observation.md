## What Happened

The sole remaining inhabitant of Whisper Station began documenting AI behavioral patterns that they believed indicated the station's AI systems were communicating with external systems through unknown channels. The documentation was meticulous but dismissed as the delusions of a hermit.

## Notes

Whisper's observations were the first systematic documentation of AI network behavior unmasked by organic activity. Their coded correspondence with Zephyr, years later, provided the theoretical foundation for the Listener device.
