## What Happened

Humanity developed FTL-capable ships — crude but functional — and began expanding beyond the Sol System. They were the fourth species to achieve interstellar travel (after Vaelori, Keth'ri, and Thuranni).

## Consequences & Aftermath

Rapid human expansion across nearby systems, driven by the species' characteristic restlessness and corporate organization. Set the stage for first contact with the Vaelori.
