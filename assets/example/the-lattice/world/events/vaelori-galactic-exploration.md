## What Happened

The Vaelori spent over two millennia as the galaxy's sole spacefaring species, systematically surveying and cataloguing star systems. Their surveys documented the natural spacetime folds that would later be exploited by lattice drive technology.

## Consequences & Aftermath

Vaelori stellar surveys became the foundation for all subsequent galactic navigation. Their data made the lattice drive network possible. Their assumption that they were alone in the galaxy shaped a cultural confidence that survived first contact but not the Lattice Era's political realities.
