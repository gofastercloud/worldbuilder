## What Happened

Lattice Systems Corp, under the second-generation Meridian Director, contracted the Keth'ri Collective to build Meridian Station — a 40-kilometer-diameter orbital station at Sol System's Lagrange point. It became the galaxy's primary trade hub and LSC's headquarters.

## Consequences & Aftermath

Meridian Station became the single most important commercial installation in the galaxy. Its AI management systems became the most critical node in the secret network.
