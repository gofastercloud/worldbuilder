## What Happened

Two brief inter-species wars between humans and Keth'ri, fought primarily over resource-rich systems in what would become the mid-region. Both wars ended inconclusively — humans had numbers and aggression, Keth'ri had manufacturing capability and collective coordination.

## Consequences & Aftermath

The wars demonstrated that inter-species conflict was economically destructive and militarily inconclusive. The subsequent Human-Keth'ri Trade Treaty established the economic partnership that would become the foundation of galactic commerce.
