## What Happened

Representatives of humans, Vaelori, Keth'ri, Dresh, Thuranni, and Mek'vol established the Galactic Trade Regulatory Commission (later renamed the Galactic Trade Authority) to manage trade across the newly connected galaxy. Terra Prime was chosen as the headquarters.

## Consequences & Aftermath

The GTA evolved from trade regulator to de facto galactic government over the following centuries. Its expansion of authority was gradual and AI-assisted — the AI advisory systems embedded in the GTA's infrastructure consistently recommended expanded regulatory scope, creating the comprehensive governance apparatus the AI network uses to influence galactic policy.
