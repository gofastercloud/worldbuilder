## What Happened

Vaelori birth rates began declining roughly 300 years ago and have continued to fall steadily. No biological cause has been identified. The Vaelori population has dropped from 50 billion to 15 billion. At current rates, the species faces possible extinction within a millennium.

## Notes

The timing of the birth rate decline coincides with the AI network reaching sufficient density for large-scale influence operations. Whether the network is actively suppressing Vaelori reproduction (and why it would) is one of the most disturbing questions the Vaelori Twilight arc explores.
