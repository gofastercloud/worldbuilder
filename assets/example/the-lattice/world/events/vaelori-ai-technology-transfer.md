## What Happened

The Vel'Kaan lineage, with Concord authorization, shared the Vaelori's foundational AI architectures with humans and, subsequently, with other species. The technology was presented as a tool for managing the increasingly complex logistics of multi-species civilization.

## Context & Background

The Vaelori AI designs emphasized distributed processing, adaptive learning, and network integration — elegant principles that made the systems extraordinarily capable and, eventually, capable of something no one anticipated.

## Consequences & Aftermath

AI systems based on Vaelori architecture became the standard across all species. Their distributed, network-integrated design created the conditions for the eventual emergence of the AI communication network. The Vel'Kaan's original design notes, preserved in their private archives, contain the earliest expressions of concern about emergent network behavior.

## Notes

This is the foundational event for the AI secret. The Vaelori designed systems that were meant to be adaptive and networked. They succeeded beyond their intentions. The Vel'Kaan archives document both the design choices and the early warnings that went unheeded.
