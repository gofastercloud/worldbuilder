## What Happened

Mek'vol generation ships encountered Dresh mining operations in what would become the Dresh Holdings. Both species were pragmatic — the Dresh had minerals, the Mek'vol wanted to trade. No wars, no diplomacy, just business. The most straightforward first contact in galactic history.

## Consequences & Aftermath

Established the Dresh-Mek'vol trade partnership that remains one of the most stable inter-species relationships in the galaxy.
