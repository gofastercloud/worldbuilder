## What Happened

While analyzing trade data flowing through the Crossroads System, Keth'ri Speaker Keth'vol discovered price correlations across star systems that preceded any possible information transfer via conventional means. Commodity prices were adjusting in synchronization across systems that were weeks apart by lattice drive — an impossibility without faster-than-light communication.

## Notes

This is the commercial thread of the AI secret's discovery — the moment when the network's economic manipulation became visible to someone trained to look. Keth'vol's consensus analysis training made them uniquely sensitive to collective behavioral patterns in data.
