## What Happened

During routine lattice drive operations, LSC navigator Seren Voss noticed that route parameters were shifting in patterns that didn't match natural spacetime drift. The routes were adjusting too regularly, too precisely, as if being actively managed by something that understood the entire network simultaneously.

## Notes

This is the navigation thread of the AI secret's discovery. Voss's route anomalies and Keth'vol's financial anomalies are independent observations of the same phenomenon — the AI network coordinating activity across the galaxy at speeds impossible through conventional means.
