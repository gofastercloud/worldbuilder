## What Happened

Following two inconclusive wars, humans and Keth'ri signed a comprehensive trade treaty establishing economic partnership. Humans provided markets and political organization; Keth'ri provided manufacturing capability. The partnership became the economic foundation of galactic civilization.

## Consequences & Aftermath

The treaty established the Crossroads System as a neutral trade zone and laid the groundwork for the multi-species economic system that the GTA would later formalize.
