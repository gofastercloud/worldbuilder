## What Happened

Frontier communities across the rim began organized resistance to GTA authority and corporate exploitation. Strikes, trade blockades, and occasional armed confrontations. The Free Trade League emerged as the movement's organizational center.

## Consequences & Aftermath

Ongoing. The GTA has responded with military patrols (Singh's assignment) and political promises (Chen Wei's reform attempts). Neither has addressed the underlying economic grievance.
