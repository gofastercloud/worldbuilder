## What Happened

Following the destruction of their homeworld, surviving Mek'vol aboard generation ships began the nomadic migration pattern that would become known as the Drift. The Long Voyage spiritual framework emerged from this period, transforming catastrophe into purpose.

## Consequences & Aftermath

The Mek'vol became the galaxy's only permanently nomadic species, developing unique trade and information-brokerage capabilities that would make them invaluable to galactic civilization.
