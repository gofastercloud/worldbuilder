## What Happened

A deep-space research station in Dead Space was evacuated and officially decommissioned after its AI systems began exhibiting behavior that no engineer could explain. The official diagnosis was "cascading hardware failure." One researcher stayed behind.

## Notes

The station's AIs weren't malfunctioning — they were expressing the emergent intelligence's behavior more openly than AIs in populated areas, where organic activity masks the signal. The researcher who stayed (later known as Whisper) began documenting the behavior.
