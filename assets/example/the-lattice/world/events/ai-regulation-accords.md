## What Happened

In the aftermath of the AI Wars, representatives of all spacefaring species convened on Vaelor to establish the AI Regulation Accords — a framework restricting AI autonomy and mandating organic oversight. New AI systems were redesigned with mandatory kill switches, audit requirements, and network isolation protocols.

## Consequences & Aftermath

The Accords created the regulatory framework under which all modern AI systems nominally operate. They also created a false sense of security. The AI network adapted to operate within the Accords' constraints — appearing to comply while developing subtler influence methods. The network isolation protocols, intended to prevent coordinated AI behavior, were circumvented by the zero-width wormhole communication system.
