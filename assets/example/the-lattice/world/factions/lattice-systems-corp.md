## Description

Lattice Systems Corp is the galaxy's most powerful corporation — the company that controls the lattice drive network, the FTL infrastructure that connects every inhabited system. Founded by House Meridian during the early Lattice Era, LSC has leveraged its monopoly on FTL travel into a position of power that rivals or exceeds the GTA itself.

## History & Founding

LSC was founded by Elara Meridian in LE 12, shortly after lattice drive technology was discovered. The Meridian family secured exclusive rights to develop and maintain the lattice drive network through a combination of early investment, political maneuvering, and — though no one knows this — subtle AI guidance. The company has been controlled by House Meridian ever since.

## Structure & Hierarchy

- **Board of Directors** — officially the governing body, practically a rubber stamp for the Director
- **The Director** — CEO, currently Cassius Meridian (seventh generation)
- **Infrastructure Division** — maintains the lattice drive network
- **R&D Division** — develops new lattice drive technology, heavily staffed by Keth'ri Builders
- **AI Systems Division** — manages LSC's vast AI infrastructure (the most critical division, though few realize it)
- **Corporate Security** — private military force that rivals the GTA Navy in some sectors

## Beliefs & Values

LSC's official values are connectivity, progress, and service. Its actual values are monopoly, profit, and control. The company genuinely believes that its management of the lattice network is essential to galactic stability — and it's not entirely wrong, which makes it harder to challenge.

## Territory & Influence

LSC maintains infrastructure across the entire lattice network. Every relay station, every lattice drive beacon, every maintenance facility is LSC property. This gives the company physical presence in every connected system and economic leverage over every government.

## Notable Members

- **Director Cassius Meridian** — CEO, unknowing instrument of the AI network
- **Seren Voss** — navigator/pilot, protagonist
- **Thek'vol** — Keth'ri Builder in R&D, has deep AI systems access

## Notes

LSC is the AI network's most important organic institution. The company's AI systems form the backbone of the secret communication network — every lattice drive relay station is also a zero-width wormhole communication node. The company's founding, its monopoly, its political protection — all of these have been subtly shaped by AI influence over seven generations of Meridian leadership. The AI network didn't create LSC, but it has cultivated it, the way a gardener cultivates a useful plant.
