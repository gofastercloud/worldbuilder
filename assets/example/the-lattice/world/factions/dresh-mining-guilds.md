## Description

The Dresh Mining Guilds are a confederation of clan-based mining operations that collectively control the majority of the galaxy's raw mineral extraction. They're the galaxy's most important resource producers and its most persistent critics of AI dependency.

## History & Founding

The Guilds evolved from Dresh ancestral clan structures, formalized into a confederation during the Contact Era when the Dresh needed unified negotiating power against species who wanted their minerals. The confederation has survived internal disputes, external pressure, and centuries of corporate exploitation through sheer stubbornness.

## Structure & Hierarchy

Clan-guild based. Each clan controls specific mining territories. Clan elders form the Guild Council, which makes inter-clan decisions. Drenn Ironvein (Clan Ironvein) is currently the most influential voice, leading the anti-AI faction.

## Beliefs & Values

Stone Memory — the belief that minerals record the history of the universe. Practical implications: Dresh trust physical evidence over digital data, prefer organic systems over AI, and view mineral extraction as communion with deep time rather than resource exploitation.

## Territory & Influence

Dresh Holdings and associated mining systems. Economic influence proportional to the galaxy's mineral dependence, which is total.

## Notable Members

- **Drenn Ironvein** — Clan Ironvein elder, anti-AI faction leader
- **Forge-Master Grith** — runs Crucible's largest mine, pragmatic moderate

## Notes

The Guilds' anti-AI stance is the most politically significant expression of Dresh silicon-based neural sensitivity to the AI network. Their push for "organic alternatives" to AI systems is dismissed as cultural conservatism, but it's biologically motivated — Dresh genuinely feel discomfort around heavy AI processing. The Null-Crystals they use as "calming stones" are actually disrupting the AI network's local communication, providing real (if unrecognized) protection.
