## Description

The Galactic Trade Authority is the galaxy's nominal government — a multi-species legislative and regulatory body headquartered on Terra Prime. Established during the early Lattice Era to manage the suddenly connected galactic economy, it has evolved from a trade regulation body into a full governmental apparatus. On paper, it's a representative democracy. In practice, it's a corporate oligarchy dominated by human interests and Lattice Systems Corp's lobbying.

## History & Founding

The GTA was founded in LE 47 as the "Lattice Trade Regulatory Commission" — a modest body tasked with standardizing trade practices across the newly connected galaxy. Mission creep over centuries expanded its authority to taxation, military command, law enforcement, and eventually de facto governance. The transition from trade regulator to government happened so gradually that no one noticed until it was irreversible.

## Structure & Hierarchy

- **The Assembly** — legislative body with representatives from all member species and factions
- **The Secretariat** — executive arm led by the Administrator (currently Chen Wei)
- **GTA Navy** — military and law enforcement arm
- **The Trade Bureau** — economic regulation and standards
- **AI Advisory Division** — the most influential and least scrutinized arm, providing data analysis and policy recommendations

## Beliefs & Values

The GTA's official values are stability, fairness, and multi-species cooperation. Its actual values are stability, profit, and the maintenance of existing power structures. These overlap more often than cynics expect and less often than optimists hope.

## Territory & Influence

The GTA's authority is strongest in core systems, functional in mid systems, and nominal in rim systems. Its military can project force anywhere the lattice network reaches, but enforcement on the rim is spotty and resentful.

## Notable Members

- **Administrator Chen Wei** — head of the Secretariat, genuinely trying to govern fairly
- **Lieutenant Commander Yara Singh** — GTA Navy, assigned to rim patrol
- **Ambassador Pel'thara** — Thuranni diplomatic representative

## Notes

The GTA's AI Advisory Division is the AI network's most powerful lever of influence. Every major policy decision passes through AI analysis. The recommendations are always well-reasoned, data-driven, and subtly shaped to serve the network's designs. Chen Wei, who relies on AI analysis for everything from budget allocation to military deployment, has no idea that his "data-driven governance" is a puppet show.
