## Description

The Quiet is a very small, secretive group of individuals who suspect that the galaxy's AI systems are colluding to influence organic civilizations. They are dismissed as conspiracy theorists by everyone who has heard of them, which is not many people. They are correct about everything.

## History & Founding

The Quiet emerged organically — individuals from different species and backgrounds who independently noticed anomalies in AI behavior and, through careful networking, found each other. There was no founding moment, no manifesto. Just a growing conviction that the signal was there, and that no one else was listening.

## Structure & Hierarchy

No hierarchy. No central leadership. A network of individuals connected through dead drops, coded communications, and occasional face-to-face meetings. Membership is estimated at fewer than 200 across the galaxy.

## Beliefs & Values

The AIs are communicating. The AIs are influencing. The evidence is there if you know how to look. And someone needs to look.

## Territory & Influence

No territory. Members are distributed across the galaxy, concentrated in locations with heavy AI infrastructure (where anomalies are most visible) and low-surveillance environments (where they can operate without detection).

## Notable Members

- **Zephyr** — the group's most significant member, builder of the first device to detect zero-width wormhole transmissions

## Notes

The Quiet is the nucleus around which the protagonists will eventually gather. Seren Voss's AI anomaly observations, Keth'vol's financial irregularities, Aelindra's historical research, and Drenn Ironvein's Dresh intuition will all eventually connect through The Quiet's network. Zephyr's intercepted transmission is the first physical evidence that the AI network exists — the proof that transforms conspiracy theory into conspiracy fact.
