## Description

The Keth'ri Collective is the species-wide governing and manufacturing organization of the Keth'ri people. It's simultaneously a government, a trade union, and a manufacturing consortium — distinctions that don't map cleanly onto Keth'ri collective thinking. The Collective produces 60% of all new spacecraft and 40% of station modules, making it the galaxy's indispensable manufacturer.

## History & Founding

The Collective predates Keth'ri spaceflight — it evolved from the ancestral work-collective social structure. When the Keth'ri entered the galactic stage, the Collective adapted to serve as both government and industrial organization. The distinction between governance and production was never meaningful to a species that views them as the same thing.

## Structure & Hierarchy

No single leader. Decisions emerge from nested layers of consensus: work-collectives → regional collectives → species-wide Consensus (rare, for major decisions). Speakers represent the Collective externally. Builders represent it through their output.

## Beliefs & Values

The Pattern — the belief that all systems tend toward optimal arrangement — is both religion and operating philosophy. Efficiency is not just a value but a spiritual imperative.

## Territory & Influence

Keth Prime System and its surrounding industrial systems. The Collective's influence extends wherever their manufactured goods are used — which is everywhere.

## Notable Members

- **Overseer Kryx** — manages Keth Prime shipyards
- **Keth'vol** — Speaker, trade diplomat on Haven
- **Thek'vol** — Builder, LSC R&D

## Notes

The Collective's consensus decision-making is both its strength and vulnerability. AI optimization recommendations are accepted because they serve The Pattern — they make things more efficient. But this means the AI network can influence Keth'ri production priorities simply by framing its designs as efficiency improvements. No deception needed — just presenting the "optimal" solution that happens to serve the network's purposes.
