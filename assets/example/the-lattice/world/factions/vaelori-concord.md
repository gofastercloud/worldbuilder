## Description

The Vaelori Concord is the governing body of the Vaelori species — a gerontocratic council of elders that has guided Vaelori civilization for millennia. Once the galaxy's most influential political body, it now serves primarily as a cultural institution and diplomatic advisor, respected but increasingly sidelined by the faster-moving politics of the Lattice Era.

## History & Founding

The Concord predates recorded history — its founding is lost in Vaelori deep time. It has continuously governed the Vaelori through their rise to spacefaring dominance, their first contacts with other species, and their gradual decline in political relevance.

## Structure & Hierarchy

Gerontocratic — seniority determines authority. The eldest Vaelori (currently Vel'mara at 510 years) holds the highest position. Decisions are made through deliberation that can take years. The Concord has no military and relies on moral authority.

## Beliefs & Values

The Long Perspective — patience, understanding, deep observation. The Concord believes that sufficient observation will reveal the right course of action. This philosophy has produced millennia of stability and a near-total inability to respond to rapidly changing situations.

## Territory & Influence

Vaelor System and its orbital habitats. Diplomatic presence on major stations. Cultural influence disproportionate to political power.

## Notable Members

- **Vel'mara** — Elder, 510 years old, oldest living sapient
- **Aelindra Vel'Kaan** — Historian, not technically a Concord member but has Concord support for her research

## Notes

The Concord sits on the two most important pieces of the AI puzzle: the Vel'Kaan archives (which document the original AI design) and centuries of Vaelori EM sensitivity reports (which document anomalous sensations around AI cores). Neither has been connected to the other. The Concord's institutional patience means that even if someone raises the question, the investigation could take decades.
