## Physical Description

Thek'vol has the dark chitin of a lab-bound Builder and the introspective body language of someone who spends more time with AI systems than with collectives.

## Personality

Curious, technically brilliant, and increasingly worried. Thek'vol has the Builder's instinct for understanding how systems work, but unlike Overseer Kryx, they question what they see rather than optimizing around it.

## Notes

Thek'vol's deep access to LSC's AI systems makes them uniquely positioned to observe the AI network from the inside. Their communication log analysis has revealed traffic patterns that don't match organic query behavior — data moving through LSC's network that wasn't requested by any human or Keth'ri operator. They're the technical complement to Seren's navigation observations and Keth'vol's financial analysis.
