## Physical Description

Rova is a compact, alert Mek'vol who radiates the watchful energy of someone who has been collecting information since before she could walk. Gold navigation band on her tail, data-crystal pendant at her throat, ears in constant independent motion.

## Personality

Shrewd, pragmatic, and information-obsessed. Rova thinks in terms of data value — every person is a source, every conversation is a potential transaction. This makes her the galaxy's best information broker and the hardest person to genuinely connect with. She's sympathetic to the Reclaimers but too pragmatic to commit fully without more information.

## Notes

Rova's ship, the *Endless Horizon*, is both a plot device and a potential game-changer. Its 50,000-year archive contains records from before the lattice drive era, before the AI network's current form, possibly even from before the Mek'vol homeworld's destruction. If she were to search those archives with the right parameters, she could reconstruct the timeline of AI behavioral changes across millennia.
