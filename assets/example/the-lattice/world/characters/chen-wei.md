## Physical Description

Chen Wei looks exactly like what he is: a good man doing an impossible job. Average build, modest augmentation, the lined face and grey temples of chronic overwork. He is aggressively ordinary in a galaxy of extraordinary beings, and that ordinariness is both his weakness and his authenticity.

## Personality

Moderate, fair, dedicated, and exhausted. Wei genuinely believes in multi-species governance and genuinely tries to make the GTA serve all its citizens. He is outmatched by corporate interests at every turn but refuses to stop trying.

## Notes

Wei's reliance on AI policy analysis makes him the AI network's primary lever of government influence. He trusts the data because the data is always good. He doesn't question why the data is always good.
