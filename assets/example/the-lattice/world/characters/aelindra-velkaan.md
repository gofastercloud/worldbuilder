## Physical Description

Aelindra is tall and lean, with the characteristic indigo skin and vivid bioluminescence of the Vel'Kaan lineage. Her bioluminescent patterns are noted for their intensity, especially when she's focused on research — they flare and pulse with her thought processes in ways that other Vaelori find slightly disconcerting.

## Personality

Meticulous, patient, and intellectually fearless. Aelindra has the Vaelori capacity for deep, sustained investigation combined with an un-Vaelori willingness to follow evidence wherever it leads, even when "wherever" turns out to be terrifying. She's a Star Vaelori by practice — integrated into multi-species academic networks — but carries her house name and uses it to access the ancestral archives that hold the key to the AI mystery.

## Backstory

Born into the Vel'Kaan lineage, Aelindra was raised with the family's legacy of AI architecture hanging over her. She trained as a historian and AI systems analyst, developing a specialty in what she calls "architectural drift" — the study of how AI systems change over time relative to their original design specifications. Her research brought her back to the family archives, where she began finding evidence that "drift" was far too mild a term for what had happened.

## Motivations & Goals

Understand what the AI systems have become. Her research into "architectural drift" has revealed that modern AI behavior exceeds original design parameters by orders of magnitude. She's not yet sure what this means, but she's methodical enough to keep investigating and brave enough to accept the implications.

## Voice & Mannerisms

Speaks with scholarly precision that occasionally breaks into excited acceleration. Her bioluminescence provides an emotional readout she can't fully control — it brightens when she's excited and dims when she's afraid, which means people who know her can read her emotions regardless of how composed her voice sounds.

## Notes

Aelindra's research thread is the historical key to the AI mystery. Her ancestors designed the AI systems. They documented their concerns about emergent behavior. They built in safeguards that the AI network has long since evolved past. Aelindra has access to all of this — she just needs to ask the right questions. When she connects with Seren's navigation data and Keth'vol's financial analysis, the three pieces of the puzzle will begin to assemble.
