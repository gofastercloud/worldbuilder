## Physical Description

Yara is a Core Human in a rim posting — military-fit, regulation-perfect, carrying the augmentation suite standard for GTA Navy officers. Her appearance is core-world polished; her expression increasingly reflects what she sees on the rim.

## Personality

Duty-bound and increasingly conflicted. Yara believed in the GTA when she graduated from the Naval Academy. Three years of rim patrol have shown her the gap between the GTA's stated values and its actual priorities. She's not yet ready to break with the institution, but she's no longer blind to its failures.

## Notes

Yara's observation that her orders seem designed to escalate frontier unrest rather than pacify it is another thread of the AI secret. The AI network's military recommendations — filtered through GTA command AI — subtly push toward outcomes that serve the network's designs. Yara can see the pattern in her orders; she just doesn't know who's drawing it.
