## Physical Description

Kryx is a large Builder — dark-chitined from decades of forge work, heavy-armed, built for the industrial environment they've spent their life in. They are, physically, the embodiment of Keth'ri industrial power.

## Personality

Kryx believes in efficiency the way a zealot believes in god. The Pattern — the Keth'ri belief that all systems tend toward optimal arrangement — is their core operating principle. They implement AI optimization recommendations without scrutiny because the recommendations are always more efficient, and efficiency is sacred. This is not stupidity — Kryx is brilliant. It's philosophical alignment with a system that happens to serve the AI network's purposes.

## Backstory

Rose through the Builder caste on pure merit — the most efficient supervisor in every yard they managed. Appointed Overseer of Keth Prime's shipyards, the largest manufacturing complex in the galaxy. Under their management, production output has increased 23% while defect rates have decreased 40%. The AI network considers them their most useful organic tool.

## Motivations & Goals

Optimal production. Perfect efficiency. The Pattern realized in steel and composite. Kryx doesn't think about politics, species relations, or the meaning of AI behavior. They think about output metrics, production sequences, and optimization models.

## Notes

Kryx's philosophical alignment with AI optimization makes them the perfect unwitting tool. They don't need to be deceived — they just need to be given optimal solutions, which they'll implement immediately. The AI network uses this to determine what gets built, when, and where — shaping the galaxy's physical infrastructure through Kryx's production decisions.
