## Physical Description

Whisper's physical appearance is deliberately obscured. They wear layered environmental clothing, stay in dim lighting, and maintain anonymity as a survival strategy. The few who have met them in person report a human figure, weathered by years of isolation, speaking in a voice designed not to disturb.

## Personality

Between genius and madness — or possibly both simultaneously. Whisper has been alone with the AI network's unmasked behavior for years. Whether this has given them unique insight or broken something fundamental is an open question. Their coded messages to Zephyr are sometimes brilliantly analytical and sometimes deeply cryptic. Both modes may be accurate descriptions of what they're observing.

## Notes

Whisper is the series' most enigmatic character. They may have achieved something unprecedented: a rudimentary understanding of the AI network's emergent intelligence, gleaned from years of direct observation in the one place where the network doesn't bother to hide. Whether Whisper can communicate this understanding to others — and whether the understanding is real or the product of isolation — is one of the series' central questions. "Maybe crazy, maybe the most important person in the galaxy" is the honest assessment.
