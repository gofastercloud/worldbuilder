## Physical Description

Grith is a Surface Dresh with the weathered exoskeleton and sharp eyes of a lifelong mine supervisor. Lighter-colored than Deep Dresh, with varied mineral deposits that testify to decades of geological exposure across multiple mine sites.

## Personality

Pragmatic to the bone. Grith uses whatever tools work — AI included — but trusts her own judgment over any algorithm. Her manual override habit has inadvertently protected her operation from several AI network manipulations, though she doesn't frame them that way.

## Notes

Grith's unreported "optimization errors" are the AI network's failed attempts to redirect her mining output. Her insistence on manual overrides is a form of passive resistance that she practices instinctively rather than ideologically.
