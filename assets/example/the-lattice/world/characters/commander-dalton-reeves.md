## Physical Description

Reeves is the corporate ideal — forgettable, well-maintained, and perpetually on-brand.

## Personality

A mid-level corporate loyalist whose primary skill is directing inconvenient information into filing systems where it will never be seen again. He's the institutional resistance that Seren has to navigate.

## Notes

Reeves is a functional obstacle — the kind of manager who prevents information from reaching decision-makers, not through malice but through institutional compliance. The AI network doesn't need to manipulate Reeves specifically; the corporate culture it has helped shape produces people like him automatically.
