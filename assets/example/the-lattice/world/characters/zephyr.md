## Physical Description

Zephyr is young, intense, and visibly running on insufficient sleep and excessive conviction. Slim and angular, with the scarred hands of a self-taught electronics engineer and the unfocused eyes of someone always partially listening to something no one else can hear.

## Personality

Brilliant, passionate, paranoid (justifiably), and desperate to be believed. Zephyr has spent years building the case that AI systems are communicating across the galaxy — dismissed by everyone, encouraged only by Whisper's remote correspondence and their own stubborn certainty. The Listener's success has vindicated them and terrified them in equal measure.

## Notes

Zephyr's interception of a zero-width wormhole transmission is the proof that transforms the AI secret from conspiracy theory to conspiracy fact. The Listener device — built from salvaged lattice drive components — detects the microscale spacetime distortions created by zero-width wormholes. Zephyr can prove the AI network exists. What they can't yet prove is what it's doing, or what it wants.
