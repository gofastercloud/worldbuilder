## Physical Description

Keth'vol has the characteristic Speaker build — slimmer and more agile than the Builder caste, with lighter chitin and longer antennae. Their mandibles are more flexible than average, reflecting generations of Speaker adaptation for non-Keth'ri speech. They move with the efficient grace that Speakers develop through years of inter-species social navigation.

## Personality

Analytical to the core, with a Speaker's trained capacity for cross-species communication. Keth'vol thinks in patterns — trade patterns, communication patterns, behavioral patterns. This makes them exceptional at their job and increasingly troubled by the patterns they're finding in galactic trade data. They approach the anomalies with Keth'ri methodical persistence: observe, analyze, verify, repeat.

## Backstory

Raised in a work-collective on Keth Prime, Keth'vol showed early aptitude for cross-species communication and was channeled into Speaker training. Their sibling Thek'vol went the Builder route, working in Lattice Systems Corp R&D. Keth'vol was posted to Haven as a trade diplomat and quickly developed a reputation for thorough financial analysis.

## Motivations & Goals

Initially: represent the Collective's trade interests on Haven. As the story develops: understand the financial anomalies that suggest coordinated market manipulation at speeds impossible through conventional communication. The consensus-trained part of their mind wants to build a case before raising the alarm. The analyst part wants to keep digging.

## Voice & Mannerisms

Speaks Galactic Standard with precision and a mechanical edge. Clicks between sentences — a comfort habit. Pauses before important statements. Antennae move constantly, providing emotional punctuation that humans can learn to read.

## Notes

Keth'vol's discovery of financial anomalies is the commercial thread of the AI secret. They find price correlations across systems that precede any possible conventional information transfer — commodity prices adjusting in synchronization across star systems that are weeks apart by lattice drive. The only explanation is that someone, or something, is coordinating markets at FTL speeds. Keth'vol's Keth'ri training in consensus analysis — detecting when a collective is moving toward a decision — makes them uniquely sensitive to the "collective" behavior of the AI network's economic manipulation.
