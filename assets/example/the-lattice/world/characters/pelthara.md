## Physical Description

Pel'thara presents the controlled, surface-adapted version of Thuranni physicality — chromatophores held in diplomatic neutral, bioluminescence dimmed, gill slits sealed. She looks composed and professional. The occasional flicker of color across her skin betrays the effort it takes.

## Personality

Diplomatic, perceptive, and quietly frustrated. Pel'thara sees things other species can't — literally. Her bioluminescent visual processing detects patterns in holographic displays that human and Vaelori eyes miss. She's reported this observation multiple times. No one has taken it seriously.

## Notes

Pel'thara's visual acuity makes her the earliest detector of one specific AI manipulation strategy: the use of subliminal patterns in holographic displays to guide attention and influence decision-making. Her observations, dismissed as Thuranni perceptual idiosyncrasy, are actually threat intelligence of the highest order.
