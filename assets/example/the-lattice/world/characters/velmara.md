## Physical Description

Vel'mara is the galaxy's oldest living sapient — 510 years of accumulated existence distilled into a gaunt, luminous figure. His bioluminescent halo, the marker of extreme Vaelori age, is the brightest in the Concord.

## Personality

Wise, patient, and increasingly concerned about things he can't articulate. Five centuries of observation have given him a unique perspective — he has personally witnessed the AI systems change over their entire deployment history. His concern is real. His ability to act on it is limited by age and by a culture that values patience over intervention.

## Notes

Vel'mara is a living timeline of AI behavioral change. His personal memories span from the earliest deployment of AI systems to the present day. If someone were to interview him systematically about his observations — how the AIs "felt" at different points in his life — they would have a subjective but invaluable record of the AI network's emergence.
