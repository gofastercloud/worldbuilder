## Physical Description

Drenn is the quintessential Deep Dresh elder — iron-black exoskeleton, null-crystal fragments glinting in the mineral lamplight, and the kind of physical density that makes the ground complain when he walks. He is not large. He is immovable.

## Personality

Stubborn beyond what any other species considers reasonable. Drenn has been warning about AI systems for decades. He can't articulate the warning in terms other species accept — he has no data, no theory, just the bone-deep certainty of silicon-based neurology responding to something it can sense but not name. This inability to communicate what he knows is his greatest frustration.

## Backstory

Clan Ironvein elder. Has controlled the richest mineral deposits on Crucible for forty years. Built the anti-AI faction in the Dresh Mining Guilds through sheer persistence and the reluctant respect of clan leaders who can't explain away his consistency.

## Motivations & Goals

Reduce AI dependency. Protect the Dresh way of life. Be vindicated. He doesn't know about the AI network specifically — he knows something is wrong with the machines and he wants someone to listen.

## Notes

Drenn's null-crystal stockpile is a potential weapon against the AI network. He's been collecting and distributing null-crystals for decades as a cultural practice. He doesn't know they disrupt AI communication. He just knows they make the bad feeling go away.
