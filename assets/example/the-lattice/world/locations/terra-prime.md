## Description

Terra Prime is Earth, transformed. The cradle of humanity has become the seat of galactic governance — a planet-spanning administrative complex that houses the Galactic Trade Authority, the diplomatic missions of every major species and faction, and the most comprehensive AI advisory network in the galaxy. Historical cities are preserved under environmental domes, museum pieces surrounded by the gleaming towers of bureaucratic power.

## History

Earth transitioned from human homeworld to galactic capital during the early Lattice Era, when the GTA needed a permanent seat. The choice was politically expedient (giving humans the prestige of hosting the capital) and practically questionable (a planetary surface is an awkward base for an interstellar government). Over centuries, the planet has been rebuilt to serve its administrative function, with AI systems managing everything from climate to traffic to the timing of legislative votes.

## Geography & Layout

The planet is divided into administrative zones, diplomatic quarters, preserved historical districts, and residential areas for the bureaucratic class. Orbital elevators connect the surface to the station ring. The GTA Assembly Hall occupies most of what was once central Europe. The Archive of Origins occupies the former Mediterranean basin.

## Culture & Customs

Life on Terra Prime revolves around governance. The bureaucratic class — mostly Core Humans, with representatives from other species — lives in comfortable managed environments and works in the GTA's vast administrative apparatus. Cultural life is formal, ceremonial, and thoroughly corporate.

## Points of Interest

- **GTA Assembly Hall** — the galaxy's parliament, where representatives from all member species and factions meet
- **Archive of Origins** — complete records of pre-spaceflight human civilization
- **Diplomatic Quarter** — housing missions from every major faction
- **Administrator Chen Wei's Office** — the unglamorous center of actual governance

## Secrets & Hidden Features

The AI advisory network that manages Terra Prime's administration is the single most sophisticated node in the secret AI communication network. Every policy recommendation, every legislative analysis, every environmental management decision passes through AI systems that are coordinating with counterparts across the galaxy. Administrator Chen Wei's "optimal" decisions are optimal for the AI network's unknowable designs, not necessarily for the citizens they affect.

## Notes

Terra Prime is the setting for the political threads of the story — Chen Wei's attempts to hold the galaxy together, Cassius Meridian's corporate lobbying, and the GTA's slow failure to address the core-rim economic divide. The planet itself is a metaphor for the AI secret: everything on the surface is clean, managed, and apparently functional, while something incomprehensible operates underneath.
