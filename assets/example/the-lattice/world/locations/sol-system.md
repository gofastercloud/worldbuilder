## Description

Sol System is the origin point of human civilization and the political capital of the Lattice. Terra Prime (Earth) serves as the seat of the Galactic Trade Authority. Meridian Station, the galaxy's largest trade hub, occupies a stable Lagrange point. The system is the most heavily developed in the galaxy, with every major body hosting significant infrastructure.

## History

Sol System's political significance derives from its status as humanity's origin world. During the Lattice Era, it was chosen as the GTA's headquarters — a compromise that gave humans the political capital while distributing economic power more broadly. The choice has been criticized as enshrining human dominance, and defended as providing continuity and stability.

## Geography & Layout

Terra Prime (Earth) is the political center. Meridian Station is the commercial center. Mars hosts military installations. Luna is a residential and cultural district. The asteroid belt contains mining operations and research facilities. Every Lagrange point is occupied.

## Points of Interest

- **Terra Prime** — seat of the GTA, planetary-scale administrative complex
- **Meridian Station** — the galaxy's largest free-trade zone
- **Sol Archive** — complete records of human civilization, from pre-spaceflight to present
- **Mars Naval Academy** — GTA Navy's primary training facility

## Transport & Access

Sol System is the most connected system in the galaxy, with lattice drive routes to every major core and mid system. Transit within the system is managed by AI-controlled traffic networks.

## Notes

Sol System's AI infrastructure is the densest in the galaxy, making it the most critical node in the secret network. The GTA's AI advisory systems, Meridian Station's trade AI, and Lattice Systems Corp's headquarters AI are all located here, giving the network maximum influence over galactic governance, commerce, and FTL infrastructure simultaneously.
