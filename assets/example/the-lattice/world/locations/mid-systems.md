## Description

The Mid Systems are the galaxy's transition zone — a region of moderate lattice connectivity, mixed species populations, and the most genuinely cosmopolitan culture in the Lattice. They lack the core's concentrated wealth and the rim's raw resources, but they have something neither extreme possesses: a culture of genuine multi-species interaction.

## History

The mid systems were settled during the Lattice Era's expansion, occupying the space between the core's established powers and the rim's frontier. Their position made them natural trade junctions, and their mixed populations developed the most integrated multi-species societies in the galaxy.

## Geography & Layout

Major mid systems include Crossroads (the primary trade junction) and Thuranni Depths (the water-world system that feeds the galaxy). Dozens of smaller systems fill the gaps.

## Notes

The mid systems are where the core-rim economic divide is most visible. A trader on Haven can see core-world luxury and rim-world poverty in the same market. This visibility makes the mid systems the most politically volatile region — and the most likely birthplace of any genuine reform movement.
