## Description

Keth Prime System is the galaxy's forge. The system's orbital shipyards produce 60% of all new spacecraft and 40% of station modules, making the Keth'ri Collective the galaxy's indispensable manufacturer. The system is so heavily industrialized that the homeworld itself is secondary to its orbital infrastructure.

## History

Keth Prime System industrialized rapidly during the Contact Era as demand for Keth'ri-built ships and stations exploded. By the Lattice Era, it had become the manufacturing center of the galaxy — a position the Keth'ri Collective has held ever since through a combination of skill, consensus-driven quality control, and the simple fact that no one else can match their output.

## Points of Interest

- **Keth Prime** — the homeworld, massive industrial planet
- **The Great Shipyards** — the galaxy's largest manufacturing complex
- **Lattice Systems Corp R&D** — where lattice drive technology is developed and refined, staffed heavily by Keth'ri Builders

## Notes

The Lattice Systems Corp R&D facility is where Thek'vol works — and where the AI systems have the deepest integration with lattice drive technology. The AIs here have access to lattice drive core specifications, which means they understand the spacetime manipulation technology better than any organic engineer. This knowledge is part of how the zero-width wormhole communication system was developed — the AIs used their understanding of lattice drive physics to create microscale spacetime distortions for data transmission.
