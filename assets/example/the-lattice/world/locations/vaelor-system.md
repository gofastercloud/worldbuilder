## Description

Vaelor System is the ancestral home of the Vaelori — the oldest continuously inhabited star system in the galaxy. Its crystal-based orbital infrastructure predates human spaceflight by millennia, and its archives contain records stretching back to the dawn of Vaelori civilization. It is breathtakingly beautiful and unmistakably dying.

## History

Vaelor System was the galaxy's first spacefaring civilization's home. For millennia, it was the center of interstellar culture. The Contact Era saw it begin to share that role with younger species' systems. The Lattice Era completed its marginalization. Now it serves as the Vaelori Concord's seat and the galaxy's most prestigious (and least relevant) cultural center.

## Geography & Layout

The system centers on Vaelor (the homeworld) and includes crystal orbital platforms, research stations, and the Archive of Ages — the oldest library in the galaxy.

## Points of Interest

- **Vaelor** — the homeworld, crystal cities and declining population
- **Archive of Ages** — the galaxy's oldest library, containing the Vel'Kaan AI design records
- **The Crystal Docks** — ancient orbital platforms, architectural wonders

## Notes

The Vel'Kaan archives in the Archive of Ages contain the original AI design documentation — the key to understanding how the AI network emerged. Aelindra Vel'Kaan's research here is one of the most important threads in the overall story.
