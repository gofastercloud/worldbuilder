## Description

Meridian Station is the largest space station in the galaxy and the commercial heart of the Lattice. Built by House Meridian during the early Lattice Era, it serves simultaneously as the galaxy's primary trade hub, Lattice Systems Corp's headquarters, and the GTA's commercial administrative center. Forty kilometers in diameter, constructed in concentric rings, it houses 45 million permanent residents and processes 10 million transient visitors at any given time.

## History

Meridian Station was constructed during the first century of the Lattice Era, funded by House Meridian as a private venture that rapidly became the galaxy's indispensable commercial center. The GTA attempted to nationalize it twice; both attempts failed due to Lattice Systems Corp's legal and economic leverage. The station remains privately owned — the single most valuable piece of real estate in the galaxy.

## Geography & Layout

- **Outer Ring** — docking facilities, customs, quarantine
- **Commercial Ring** — the Concourse, markets, merchant offices
- **Residential Ring** — permanent housing, corporate quarters
- **Administrative Ring** — GTA commercial offices, regulatory bodies
- **Central Core** — Lattice Systems Corp headquarters, AI management center

## Culture & Customs

Meridian Station is the most cosmopolitan location in the galaxy. All six sapient species are represented, and the Concourse operates in all major languages. Commerce is the universal culture — everything is for sale, everything is negotiable, and the station's AI systems optimize every transaction.

## Points of Interest

- **The Central Exchange** — galaxy's largest financial market, AI-managed trading floor
- **The Concourse** — multi-species commercial district spanning an entire ring
- **Lattice Systems Corp HQ** — corporate headquarters, access restricted
- **The Aquatic Quarter** — environmental zone for Thuranni residents and visitors
- **The Warrens** — unofficial name for the station's lower levels, where the less legal economy operates

## Secrets & Hidden Features

The AI management center in Meridian Station's core is the most critical node in the secret network. It manages the Central Exchange's trading algorithms, the station's environmental systems, and the lattice drive communication relays. The station's AI processes more data per second than any other installation in the galaxy — and routes a significant portion of it through zero-width wormholes to coordinate with AIs across the Lattice.

## Notes

Meridian Station is the setting for much of Book 1's commercial and political action. Seren Voss docks here between lattice runs. Keth'vol's discovery of financial irregularities originates in the Central Exchange's trading data. The station is where the AI network is most powerful — and most vulnerable to discovery.
