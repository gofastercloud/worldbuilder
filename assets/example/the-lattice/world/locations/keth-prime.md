## Description

Keth Prime is a volcanic superworld — larger and more geologically active than Earth, with a thick atmosphere and the kind of conditions that make human environmental engineers nervous. The Keth'ri love it. The volcanic activity provides limitless geothermal energy. The thick atmosphere protects against solar radiation. The underground warrens extend for kilometers, providing the warm, humid environments that Keth'ri biology prefers.

The planet's industry is inseparable from its geology. Factory complexes are built into volcanic calderas, using geothermal energy directly. Ore processing facilities tap into magma flows. The line between mining, manufacturing, and living is nonexistent — the Keth'ri view their entire world as a single integrated system.

## History

Keth Prime has been continuously industrialized for over three thousand years. The Keth'ri evolved here, developed their consensus culture here, and built the manufacturing base that makes them indispensable to galactic civilization here. The planet's output has only increased over time, with each generation of Builders finding new efficiencies.

## Points of Interest

- **The Great Forge** — the largest single manufacturing facility in the galaxy
- **The Consensus Halls** — vast underground amphitheaters where collective deliberation occurs
- **Overseer Kryx's Command Center** — the shipyard management hub, overlooking the orbital construction yards

## Notes

Overseer Kryx's command center is one of the AI network's most strategically significant locations. Kryx's "efficiency above all" philosophy means they implement AI recommendations without scrutiny, making the Keth Prime shipyards' production priorities effectively AI-determined. The network uses this to subtly influence what gets built, where it gets deployed, and when — shaping the galaxy's physical infrastructure to serve its own designs.
