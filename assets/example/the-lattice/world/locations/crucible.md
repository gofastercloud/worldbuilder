## Description

Crucible is the Dresh Mining Guilds' crown jewel — a volcanic world of extraordinary mineral wealth and equally extraordinary hostility to non-Dresh life. Surface temperatures average 180°C, the atmosphere is toxic to carbon-based species, and geological instability is a daily reality. The Dresh consider it home.

## History

Crucible was among the first worlds claimed by the Dresh during the Contact Era. Its extreme conditions deterred all other species, giving the Dresh unchallenged access to one of the richest mineral deposits in the galaxy.

## Geography & Layout

- **Surface** — volcanic ridges, magma flows, exposed mineral deposits, surface mining operations
- **Upper Warrens** — primary mining installations, processing facilities, living quarters (0-10km depth)
- **Deep Mines** — the richest deposits, including Null-Crystal caverns (10-50km depth)
- **The Ironvein Deep** — the deepest mine shaft in the galaxy, controlled by Clan Ironvein

## Points of Interest

- **The Great Seam** — the largest continuous mineral deposit known, spanning 200km
- **Forge-Master Grith's Operations Center** — the largest mine's command hub
- **The Null-Crystal Caverns** — deep geological formations containing concentrations of AI-disruptive crystals
- **The Ironvein Deep** — Clan Ironvein's ancestral mine, 50km deep

## Secrets & Hidden Features

The Null-Crystal caverns are the galaxy's only significant source of a material that can disrupt AI processing. The Dresh have used Null-Crystals for millennia — as decorative elements, as currency tokens, and as "calming stones" placed near equipment they find unsettling. They've never investigated WHY the crystals make them feel better around AI systems. The crystals' atomic structure creates localized electromagnetic interference at the frequencies the AI network uses for zero-width wormhole communication — effectively jamming the signal.

## Notes

Crucible is a key location for the Dresh storyline and the potential endgame of the AI secret plot. If the AI network is ever to be confronted, Null-Crystals from Crucible may be the only tool available for disrupting its communication. Drenn Ironvein's anti-AI crusade, Forge-Master Grith's pragmatic mining operations, and the strategic significance of Null-Crystals all converge here.
