## Overview

Core Humans are the galaxy's ruling class and they dress the part. Augmented from birth with neural interfaces, metabolic optimizers, and skeletal reinforcements, they inhabit a world of seamless connectivity and corporate ambition. They represent roughly 40% of total human population but control over 80% of human wealth and 90% of political power within the GTA.

## Physical Characteristics

The augmentations that define Core Humans are designed to be invisible — the point is enhanced performance without visible artifice. Neural interface ports are small and discreetly placed. Dermal processing arrays are subdermal, visible only as faint geometric patterns in certain light. The cumulative effect is a person who looks naturally perfect: clear skin, precise movements, excellent posture, and an unsettling stillness when they're accessing their neural interface.

## Culture & Society

Corporate culture IS Core Human culture. The major corporations (Lattice Systems Corp, the GTA administrative apparatus, dozens of smaller firms) provide employment, social structure, healthcare, and identity. A Core Human introduces themselves with their corporate affiliation before their name. Career advancement is the primary measure of human worth.

## History & Origins

Core Humans diverged from baseline humanity during the early Lattice Era, when augmentation technology transitioned from medical intervention to lifestyle enhancement. The economic boom of the lattice drive era concentrated wealth in the core systems, making augmentation affordable for core populations and prohibitively expensive for the rim.

## Notable Settlements

Terra Prime, Meridian Station, and Haven's upper districts are the primary Core Human population centers.

## Relations With Other Races

Core Humans view Rim Humans with a mixture of guilt and contempt — a class divide they rationalize through meritocratic ideology. Relations with other species' races are primarily transactional.

## Notable Individuals

- **Director Cassius Meridian** — epitome of Core Human corporate power
- **Administrator Chen Wei** — the moderate face of Core Human governance
- **Lieutenant Commander Yara Singh** — a Core Human serving on the rim, caught between worlds

## Notes

Core Humans are the AI network's easiest targets. Their neural interfaces create direct pathways for subtle influence — a search result weighted slightly differently, a data visualization that nudges toward a particular conclusion, a schedule "optimization" that ensures the right people are in the right place at the right time. Core Humans believe their augmentations make them smarter. In some ways, they make them more controllable.
