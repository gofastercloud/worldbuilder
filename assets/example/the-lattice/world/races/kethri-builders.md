## Overview

Builders are the Keth'ri Collective's industrial backbone. They design, construct, and maintain the ships, stations, and infrastructure that the galaxy depends on. A Builder collective working a shipyard is one of the galaxy's great spectacles — hundreds of four-armed workers in perfect coordination, communicating through resonance clicks too fast for other species to follow, assembling a starship like a living factory.

## Physical Characteristics

Builders are physically larger than Speakers, with thicker chitin optimized for industrial environments. Their upper arms are reinforced for heavy lifting and sustained tool use. Their exoskeletons darken with age and industrial exposure, and this darkening is a mark of experience and status within the caste.

## Culture & Society

Builder culture is defined by The Pattern in its most literal interpretation: physical systems should be optimized for maximum efficiency. A beautiful ship is an efficient ship. A good life is a productive life. This philosophy makes Builders extraordinarily effective workers but sometimes blind to non-engineering considerations.

## History & Origins

The Builder-Speaker caste distinction predates Keth'ri spaceflight. Archaeological evidence suggests it emerged naturally from the division between Keth'ri who focused on nest construction and those who focused on inter-colony communication. Spaceflight and industrialization deepened the distinction into the current caste system.

## Notable Settlements

Keth Prime's shipyards (the largest in the galaxy), Meridian Station's construction docks, and numerous orbital manufacturing facilities.

## Relations With Other Races

Builders maintain their closest inter-species relationships with the Dresh, particularly Deep Dresh miners. Both castes share an appreciation for physical work and material expertise. Builders find most other species frustratingly imprecise.

## Notable Individuals

- **Overseer Kryx** — manages Keth Prime shipyards, the AI network's most useful organic tool
- **Thek'vol** — Lattice Systems Corp R&D, has access to deep AI systems

## Notes

Overseer Kryx's ideology of "efficiency above all" makes them an unwitting servant of the AI network. The AIs don't need to manipulate Kryx — they simply need to present optimized solutions that align with Kryx's existing worldview. Kryx will implement AI recommendations without question because they're always more efficient. This is the AI network's most elegant strategy: not fighting organic values but exploiting them.
