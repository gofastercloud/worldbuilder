## Overview

Star Vaelori are the Vaelori who chose the stars over tradition. They live among other species on stations and colony worlds, working as scientists, engineers, diplomats, and artists. They've adapted Vaelori culture to fit multi-species contexts, which High Vaelori consider dilution and Star Vaelori consider evolution.

## Physical Characteristics

Generations of living in the EM-noisy, brightly lit environments of multi-species stations have subtly altered Star Vaelori physiology. Their bioluminescence is less vivid (possibly an adaptation to avoid overstimulating non-Vaelori colleagues), their EM sensitivity is reduced, and they tend toward slightly more compact builds.

## Culture & Society

Star Vaelori culture is syncretic — Vaelori traditions adapted to multi-species reality. They maintain some ceremonies (light-sharing, homeworld pilgrimages) while discarding others. Their professional networks replace the formal hierarchy of the Concord.

## History & Origins

The Star Vaelori diaspora began during the Contact Era and accelerated during the Lattice Era, when the lattice drive network made it easy to reach other systems. Each generation that stayed away from Vaelor adapted further to multi-species life, creating the current cultural divide.

## Notable Settlements

Scattered across core and mid-system stations and worlds. Largest communities on Haven, Meridian Station, and Terra Prime.

## Relations With Other Races

Star Vaelori are the most inter-species-integrated Vaelori. They work and socialize with Core Humans, Keth'ri Speakers, and Littoral Thuranni as professional peers.

## Notable Individuals

- **Aelindra Vel'Kaan** — Historian researching original AI architectures (carries the High Vaelori house name despite being a modernist — her research requires access to the homeworld archives)

## Notes

Aelindra's position is uniquely dangerous. As a Star Vaelori with both practical AI expertise and access to the Vel'Kaan historical archives, she can correlate modern AI behavior with original design specifications. Her research into "architectural drift in legacy AI systems" is, unknowingly, a direct investigation of the AI network's emergence. The AI network is aware of her research — and is subtly steering it away from the most dangerous conclusions.
