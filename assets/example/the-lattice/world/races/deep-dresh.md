## Overview

Deep Dresh are the Dresh in their purest form — clan-guild miners who live in the volcanic depths of worlds like Crucible, extracting the raw materials the galaxy runs on. They are the most vocal opponents of AI dependency in the galaxy, and they have the most biologically valid reason for their suspicion, even though they can't explain it in terms other species would accept.

## Physical Characteristics

Adapted to extreme subterranean environments, Deep Dresh are darker, denser, and more heat-resistant than Surface Dresh. Their tremorsense — the ability to detect vibrations through solid material — is acute enough to map tunnel systems by feel alone. Their mineral-enriched exoskeletons are functionally armor-plated.

## Culture & Society

Deep Dresh clan-guilds are the most traditional Dresh social units. Stone Memory practices are daily rituals, not cultural artifacts. AI systems are used only when absolutely necessary, with organic backups for every critical system. Deep Dresh mining operations run on expertise, tradition, and sheer physical resilience.

## History & Origins

Deep Dresh represent the original Dresh way of life — subterranean mining in harsh environments. When Surface Dresh adapted to multi-species trade, Deep Dresh stayed underground. The divergence has deepened over generations.

## Notable Settlements

Crucible's deep mining complexes, the Ironvein Holds, various mining operations across the Dresh Holdings.

## Relations With Other Races

Deep Dresh interact primarily with Keth'ri Builders (who buy their raw materials) and Mek'vol traders (who distribute their goods). They avoid Core Humans and view GTA representatives with open hostility.

## Notable Individuals

- **Drenn Ironvein** — Clan Ironvein elder, leads the anti-AI faction

## Notes

Drenn Ironvein's anti-AI crusade is the most politically significant expression of Deep Dresh instinct. He can't explain WHY AI systems feel wrong — his arguments are dismissed as superstition and Luddism — but his silicon-based neurology is genuinely responding to the AI network's zero-width wormhole communication. If he ever connects with someone who can provide the theoretical framework (like Zephyr or Aelindra), the combination of Dresh intuition and scientific analysis could crack the secret open.
