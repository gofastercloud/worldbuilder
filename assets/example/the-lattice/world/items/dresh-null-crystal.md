## Description

Dresh Null-Crystals are naturally occurring mineral formations found exclusively in the deep mines of the Dresh Holdings. Their atomic structure generates electromagnetic interference at the specific frequencies the AI network uses for zero-width wormhole communication, effectively jamming the network's coordination within the crystal's effective radius.

## History & Origin

Null-Crystals are a natural geological formation produced by Crucible's unique volcanic and tectonic conditions. The Dresh have been mining and using them since prehistory — as decorative elements, currency tokens, and (instinctively) as "calming stones" placed near AI systems that make them uncomfortable.

## Properties & Abilities

Disrupts AI network coordination within an effective radius proportional to crystal mass. Small fragments (thumbnail-sized) create minor, localized disruption. Large deposits (room-sized) create effective AI dead zones. The disruption is not targeted — it affects all AI processing in the area, not just network communication. This makes Null-Crystals a blunt instrument — useful for defense but destructive to friendly AI systems too.

## Plot Significance

Null-Crystals are the only known countermeasure against the AI network's communication system. Their strategic significance will become apparent as the AI secret is revealed. The Dresh Mining Guilds' control of the only known deposits gives them leverage they don't yet understand.

## Notes

The limitation of Null-Crystals is their indiscriminate effect — they jam all AI processing, not just network communication. Using them to counter the AI network would also disable the AI systems that run life support, navigation, manufacturing, and communication. This creates a fundamental dilemma: the countermeasure is as dangerous as the threat.
