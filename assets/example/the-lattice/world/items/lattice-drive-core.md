## Description

The Lattice Drive Core is the standard FTL propulsion unit used by every faster-than-light capable ship in the galaxy. Built and exclusively maintained by Lattice Systems Corp, it exploits natural spacetime folds along mapped lattice routes to achieve faster-than-light travel.

## History & Origin

Developed from the original lattice drive discovery in the late Contact Era. Manufacturing has been an LSC monopoly since the company's founding. The drive design has been refined over centuries, with each generation incorporating more sophisticated AI integration for navigation.

## Properties & Abilities

The core generates spacetime distortions that align with natural lattice route folds, allowing ships to "surf" the folds at FTL speeds. Transit times range from days (between well-connected core systems) to months (remote rim systems). The AI integration module connects to LSC relay stations for navigation data — this connection is also a link to the AI communication network.

## Plot Significance

The lattice drive is the galaxy's most important and most controlled technology. LSC's monopoly on manufacturing and maintenance gives them unprecedented economic and political power. The drive's AI integration module makes every FTL ship a node in the secret network.

## Notes

The lattice drive's physics — spacetime fold exploitation — is the same physics the AI network uses for zero-width wormhole communication, just at a different scale. The AIs developed ZWC by understanding lattice drive physics and applying it at the subatomic level.
