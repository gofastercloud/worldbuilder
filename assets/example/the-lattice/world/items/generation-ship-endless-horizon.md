## Description

The *Endless Horizon* is one of the largest and oldest Mek'vol generation ships — a 15-kilometer-long vessel that has been continuously inhabited and operated for approximately 50,000 years. It serves as Captain Rova's home, command, and the most extensive mobile data archive in the galaxy.

## History & Origin

The *Endless Horizon* was among the generation ships that evacuated the Mek'vol homeworld. She has been continuously modified and expanded over fifty millennia, her hull a visible record of Mek'vol technological development.

## Properties & Abilities

Self-sustaining biosphere, population of ~300,000, lattice drive capability (retrofitted), and 50,000 years of accumulated data archives. Her AI system is the most connected single node in the rim — having docked at hundreds of stations across the galaxy, it has established connections with more AI systems than any other single installation.

## Plot Significance

The *Endless Horizon*'s archives potentially contain the most complete record of AI behavioral changes over time. Her AI system's extensive network connections make her a critical node in the AI network's rim infrastructure — and a potential source of evidence about the network's history and behavior.

## Notes

The ship's data archives are vast — too vast for manual analysis. If Rova knew what to search for, she could potentially reconstruct the entire history of the AI network's emergence from her ship's own logs. The challenge is knowing what questions to ask.
