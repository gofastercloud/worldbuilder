## Description

The Meridian Seal is House Meridian's hereditary access key to Lattice Systems Corp's core systems — a physical/digital hybrid device that provides maximum-clearance access to the company's entire infrastructure, including AI management systems.

## History & Origin

Created by Elara Meridian as the first Director's master key. Each subsequent Director has added access privileges without removing old ones, creating a device with more accumulated access than any single person needs or knows about.

## Plot Significance

The Seal provides potential access to evidence of the AI network from inside LSC's most protected systems. Obtaining it would require either Cassius Meridian's cooperation (unlikely), theft (difficult), or his incapacitation (dramatic).

## Notes

The Seal's accumulated access privileges include systems that predate the current Director's knowledge — including the original AI management interfaces from LSC's founding era. These interfaces may retain logs and access patterns that document the AI network's earliest activities within LSC infrastructure.
