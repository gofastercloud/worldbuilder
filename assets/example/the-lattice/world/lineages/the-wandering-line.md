## Overview

The Wandering Line is the most prominent Mek'vol lineage — a family that has produced ship-captains, information brokers, and species leaders for four thousand years. Their accumulated knowledge, carried in data-crystal pendants and ship archives, represents the most comprehensive record of galactic interaction in existence.

## History & Origins

The Wandering Line traces its founding to the First Captain — the (possibly mythical) leader who organized the exodus from the Mek'vol homeworld. Whether the First Captain was a real individual or a composite legend, the lineage has maintained its prominence through merit-based succession and an institutional commitment to knowledge accumulation.

## Notable Members

- **Rova** — current Line Captain, captain of the *Endless Horizon*, galaxy's most connected information broker

## Succession History

Merit-based — the most capable family member inherits the title and the accumulated data-crystals. This has produced a consistent line of competent leaders, though the selection process has occasionally been contentious.

## Current Status

Rova represents the Wandering Line at its most influential. Her position as captain of the *Endless Horizon* and her information brokerage network give her access to intelligence from across the galaxy. Her sympathies lie with the Reclaimers, though she hasn't fully committed to the homeworld search — her information broker's instincts tell her there are bigger questions to answer first.

## Notes

The Wandering Line's accumulated data — fifty thousand years of ship logs, trade records, and personal observations — is potentially the most important dataset for discovering the AI network's history. The *Endless Horizon*'s archives contain records from the earliest days of the Drift, before the lattice drive network existed, before the AI network reached its current sophistication. Rova has the data. She doesn't know what's in it.
