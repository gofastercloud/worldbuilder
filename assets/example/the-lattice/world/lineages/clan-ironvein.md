## Overview

Clan Ironvein is the most powerful Dresh mining dynasty — controllers of the richest mineral deposits on Crucible and leaders of the anti-AI movement within the Dresh Mining Guilds. Their ancestral territory includes the deepest mine shafts in the galaxy and, critically, the largest known null-crystal deposits.

## History & Origins

The Ironvein clan has been mining Crucible since the Pre-Contact era — one of the oldest continuous operations of any kind in the galaxy. Their wealth comes from iron, rare metals, and increasingly, null-crystals — a resource whose true value no one yet understands.

## Notable Members

- **Drenn Ironvein** — current Clan Elder, anti-AI faction leader, the most prominent voice calling for reduced AI dependency

## Bloodline Traits

Ironvein family members exhibit the darkest exoskeletons among the Dresh (heavy iron content), the strongest tremorsense, and the most pronounced negative reaction to AI systems. This last trait — a persistent feeling of unease around AI processing cores — has been the clan's defining characteristic for generations and is biologically real, not superstitious.

## Alliances & Rivalries

Primary rival: House Meridian (by proxy through LSC). Allied with the Free Trade League through shared anti-corporate interests. Respected within the Dresh Mining Guilds as the most traditional and successful clan.

## Current Status

At the height of their influence within Dresh society. Drenn Ironvein's anti-AI campaign has gained traction as AI anomalies become more frequent. The clan's control of null-crystal deposits gives them a strategic resource they don't yet know is strategic.

## Notes

Clan Ironvein's null-crystal deposits are the key to any potential endgame against the AI network. The crystals' ability to disrupt AI processing at the frequencies used for zero-width wormhole communication means they could, theoretically, be used to jam or disrupt the network's coordination. Drenn Ironvein has been stockpiling null-crystals for years as a cultural practice. He doesn't know he's been building a weapon.
