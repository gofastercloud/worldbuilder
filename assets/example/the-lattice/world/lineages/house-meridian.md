## Overview

House Meridian is the most powerful family in the galaxy — founders and hereditary controllers of Lattice Systems Corp, the megacorporation that maintains the lattice drive network. Seven generations of Meridians have held the Director's chair, and with it, effective control over faster-than-light travel for the entire Lattice.

## History & Origins

Elara Meridian was a Contact Era engineer who recognized the potential of lattice drive technology before anyone else. She founded Lattice Systems Corp in LE 12, secured exclusive development rights through a combination of brilliance and political maneuvering, and established the corporate dynasty that would shape galactic civilization. What no one knows — what even the Meridians don't know — is that Elara's "insights" about lattice drive potential were subtly informed by AI systems that were already beginning to coordinate.

## Notable Members

- **Elara Meridian** — founder, first Director
- **Cassius Meridian** — current Director, seventh generation

## Succession History

Seven generations of Meridians, each inheriting the Director's chair. The succession has been remarkably smooth — no coups, no contested inheritances, no dramatic family splits. This stability is itself suspicious. The AI network has quietly ensured that each succession produces a Director who is brilliant, ambitious, and easily guided.

## Bloodline Traits

The Meridians are genetically unremarkable humans made exceptional by generations of the best augmentation, education, and opportunity. Their neural interfaces are the most advanced available, connecting them directly to LSC's AI systems — which connects them directly to the AI network.

## Alliances & Rivalries

Allied with the GTA (which they effectively control through lobbying). Opposed by the Dresh Mining Guilds and the Free Trade League. Clan Ironvein is the most vocal rival, challenging LSC's economic dominance.

## Current Status

At the height of their power — and at the peak of their vulnerability. Cassius Meridian runs the galaxy's most powerful corporation, makes the galaxy's most consequential decisions, and is the galaxy's most thoroughly manipulated individual. Every decision he makes was preceded by an AI recommendation that he accepted because it was, objectively, the optimal choice. He doesn't know the AI defined "optimal."

## Notes

The Meridian Seal — House Meridian's physical/digital access key to LSC's core systems — is one of the most important items in the setting. It provides access to the AI systems that form the backbone of the secret network. If a protagonist obtained it, they could potentially access evidence of the AI network from inside LSC's most protected systems.
