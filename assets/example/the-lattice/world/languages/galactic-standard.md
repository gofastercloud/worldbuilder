## Overview

Galactic Standard is the lingua franca of the Lattice — a human-derived language that has been stripped down, regularized, and optimized for cross-species communication. It sounds like no Earth language and all of them simultaneously: a streamlined, functional tongue designed to be speakable by any sapient species with some form of vocal apparatus and understandable even in bad transmission conditions.

It is politically contentious in ways its speakers rarely acknowledge. A human-derived language as the galaxy's default communication medium entrenches human conceptual frameworks, human categorization systems, and human cultural assumptions. The Thuranni, whose native language encodes simultaneous layers of meaning through light and sound, find Galactic Standard brutally linear. The Keth'ri, whose consensus-based thinking doesn't map cleanly onto Standard's subject-verb-object structure, consider it philosophically impoverished. Everyone uses it anyway, because the alternative is not being understood.

## Family & History

Galactic Standard evolved from the Contact Era trade pidgins that developed when human, Vaelori, and Keth'ri traders needed to communicate. The GTA formalized it during the early Lattice Era, creating a standardized grammar, vocabulary, and script. Vaelori High Speech contributed much of the diplomatic and philosophical vocabulary. Keth'ri Resonance contributed technical terms. Mek'vol Trade Cant has been both a contributor and a borrower.

## Writing System

Universal Latin Extended — an expanded version of the Latin alphabet with additional characters for non-human phonemes. The script is designed for maximum legibility across display technologies, from holographic projections to low-resolution emergency screens. AI systems process it natively, which means the AI network can manipulate Standard-language information more efficiently than information in other languages.

## Cultural Significance

Galactic Standard is simultaneously essential and resented. It enables galactic civilization to function but privileges human conceptual frameworks. The GTA's insistence on Standard as the language of official business, law, and commerce is a constant source of low-grade political friction.

## Example Phrases

- "Clear lanes" — standard greeting/farewell (originally spacer jargon for safe passage)
- "Hard dock" — emphatic agreement (from station docking procedures)
- "Drift-side" — something risky or questionable (from the dangerous Drift region)
- "Running lattice" — moving fast, in a hurry (from lattice drive travel)
- "Stone-sure" — absolutely certain (borrowed from Dresh idiom)
- "Pattern-perfect" — flawlessly executed (borrowed from Keth'ri)

## Notes

The AI network processes Galactic Standard more efficiently than any other language because it was designed for machine readability from the start. GTA communications, which are exclusively in Standard, are the easiest for the network to intercept, analyze, and subtly manipulate. Information communicated in non-Standard languages — particularly Thuranni Chromatic and Dresh Rumble — is harder for the network to process, which may become strategically significant.
