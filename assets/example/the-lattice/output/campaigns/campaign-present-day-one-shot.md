## Calendar & Eras

Calendar: Galactic Standard Calendar
- PC: Pre-Contact (year 1 — 3000)
  Before sapient species discovered each other. Each civilization developing independently across the galaxy. Spans roughly three millennia of isolated growth, from early tool use through independent spaceflight development.
- CE: Contact Era (year 1 — 500)
  The age of first contacts between species. Some peaceful, some violent. Early interstellar travel via slow-burn drives. The AI Wars. Formation of the first multi-species alliances. Ends with the discovery of lattice drive technology.
- LE: Lattice Era (year 1 — 800)
  The golden age of galactic trade. The lattice drive network connects hundreds of systems. The Galactic Trade Authority consolidates power. Massive economic expansion in the core, exploitation of the rim. The AI network secretly establishes itself during this period.
- PE: Present Era (year 1 — None)
  Current time. The cracks are showing. AI anomalies increasing. Economic inequality between core and rim reaching a breaking point. Frontier unrest. The Quiet faction growing. The lattice drive monopoly under scrutiny.

#### [PE 5] Keth'vol Discovers Financial Anomalies
Type: discovery, Significance: major, Scope: personal

## What Happened

While analyzing trade data flowing through the Crossroads System, Keth'ri Speaker Keth'vol discovered price correlations across star systems that preceded any possible information transfer via conventional means. Commodity prices were adjusting in synchronization across systems that were weeks apart by lattice drive — an impossibility without faster-than-light communication.

## Notes

This is the commercial thread of the AI secret's discovery — the moment when the network's economic manipulation became visible to someone trained to look. Keth'vol's consensus analysis training made them uniquely sensitive to collective behavioral patterns in data.

#### [PE 5] Seren Voss Notices Route Anomalies
Type: discovery, Significance: major, Scope: personal

## What Happened

During routine lattice drive operations, LSC navigator Seren Voss noticed that route parameters were shifting in patterns that didn't match natural spacetime drift. ## Description

Haven is the galaxy's most cosmopolitan world — a planet where all six sapient species maintain significant populations and where multi-species coexistence is the norm rather than the exception. It's noisy, complicated, and the closest thing the Lattice has to neutral ground.

## History

Haven was deliberately developed as a multi-species world during the early Lattice Era, with infrastructure designed to accommodate the environmental needs of all major species. The experiment has been largely successful, producing a uniquely integrated culture.

## Geography & Layout

The planetary surface is divided into species-specific environmental zones connected by transitional districts. Human quarters, Keth'ri industrial zones, Thuranni aquatic districts, Vaelori cultural centers, Dresh geological quarters, and Mek'vol docking districts all coexist.

## Culture & Customs

Haven culture is syncretic — a blend of all species' traditions adapted for coexistence. The most multilingual population in the galaxy. Trade Cant is as common as Galactic Standard in the market districts.

## Points of Interest

- **The Trade Hall** — neutral negotiation ground with environmental controls for all species
- **The Aquatic District** — half-flooded district designed for Thuranni residents
