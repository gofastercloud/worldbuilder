## Overview
High Eldari is the formal language of the Eldari people — beautiful, complex, and dying. Fewer speakers remain each century. It has magical resonance: Weave spells spoken in High Eldari are measurably stronger.

## Family & History
Descended from Proto-Eldari, the theoretical first language. Wood Eldari diverged around 1A 200 when the forest-dwelling Eldari developed their own dialect. After the Sundering, High Eldari became a scholarly and ritual language as most Eldari learned Common for trade.

## Example Phrases
- "Vel'ashan" — "go in peace" (formal farewell)
- "Kel'thara" — "the Weave remembers" (expression of wonder or grief)
- "Ash'velen mor" — "truth in shadow" (said when revealing a secret)

## Notes
Magical incantations in High Eldari are approximately 15% more effective than the same spell in Common. This is measurable and documented by the Silver Order.
