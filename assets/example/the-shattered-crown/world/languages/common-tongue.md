## Overview
The Common Tongue is the lingua franca of the post-Sundering world. Nearly everyone speaks it, though accent and dialect vary enormously between Valdrian city-dwellers and Steppe Folk riders.

## Family & History
Descended from Old Common, the language of pre-Sundering human settlements. Heavily influenced by High Eldari scholarly vocabulary (most magical and academic terms are Eldari loanwords) and Deepstone trade terms (metallurgy, mining, and craft words).

## Example Phrases
- "Steel and sun" — common greeting (short for "may your steel be sharp and the sun warm")
- "Crown's truth" — oath/affirmation ("I swear on the crown")
- "Wind take it" — mild oath of frustration
