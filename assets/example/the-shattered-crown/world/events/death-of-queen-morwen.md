## What Happened
In the final battle of the War of Broken Crowns, Lord Vexis confronted Queen Morwen in the throne room of Valdris. She chose to shatter the Crown rather than let him claim it, and Vexis struck her down.

## Consequences & Aftermath
Kael arrived moments too late. Her death galvanized the Silver Order for the final push that defeated Vexis.
