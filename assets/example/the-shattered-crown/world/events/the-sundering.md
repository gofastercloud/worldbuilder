## What Happened
The ancient lord Vexis attempted to claim the Crown of Ages, unleashing a magical cataclysm that shattered the continent and ended the Age of Dawn.

## Context & Background
The gods had been withdrawing from the world for centuries. Vexis believed the Crown could bind their power to mortal hands.

## Consequences & Aftermath
The continent split into the Five Realms. The Age of Crowns began. Magic became unstable and rare.
