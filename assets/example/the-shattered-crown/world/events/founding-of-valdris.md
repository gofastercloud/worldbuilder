## What Happened
Queen Morwen gathered the survivors of the Sundering and founded the city of Valdris in the sheltered valley between the Shattered Peaks.

## Consequences & Aftermath
Valdris became the seat of power for the Second Age. The Silver Order was established as its guardians.
