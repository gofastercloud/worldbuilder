## What Happened
Lord Vexis returned, seeking the Crown of Ages once more. The Silver Order, led by Kael Dawnblade, defended Valdris through seven years of brutal warfare.

## Consequences & Aftermath
The Silver Order prevailed but at terrible cost. Queen Morwen was slain. The Crown was shattered into five fragments.
