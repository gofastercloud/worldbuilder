## Overview
House Dawnblade is a minor noble house of Valdris, known for producing outstanding soldiers and Silver Order commanders. Their faint Weave sensitivity is unusual for humans and the subject of quiet speculation.

## History & Origins
Founded by Aldric Dawnblade, a common soldier who distinguished himself during border skirmishes. He was granted a minor title and took the surname Dawnblade after his habit of drilling at first light.

## Notable Members
- **Aldric Dawnblade** — Founder, first Commander from the house
- **Sera Dawnblade** — Aldric's wife, a healer
- **Kael Dawnblade** — Current head, protagonist, witnessed the death of Queen Morwen
