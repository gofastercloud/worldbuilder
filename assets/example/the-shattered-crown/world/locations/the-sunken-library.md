## Description
Half-submerged in the coastal marshes, the Library predates the Sundering. Its upper levels are picked clean, but the flooded lower vaults hold secrets no living person has read.

## History
Built during the Age of Dawn. Partially destroyed during the Sundering when the coastline shifted. Rediscovered in 2A 45.

## Geography & Layout
Three levels above water, at least five below. The deeper you go, the more intact — and more dangerous — it becomes.

## Culture & Customs
A small community of scholars lives in the upper levels. Entry to the lower vaults requires Silver Order authorization.

## Points of Interest
- The Reading Hall: Upper level, partially restored
- The Weave Gate: Connects to Valdris (hidden)
- The Deep Vaults: Flooded, sealed, rumoured to contain pre-Sundering artifacts

## Transport & Access
Reachable from Valdris via the King's Road (4 days by horse) or instantly via the hidden Weave Gate. No other major routes connect here.

## Secrets & Hidden Features
The deepest vault contains a fragment of the Crown of Ages, though the Silver Order doesn't know this yet.
