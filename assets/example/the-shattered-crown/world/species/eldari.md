## Overview
The Eldari are the oldest sapient species, with racial memory stretching back to before the Sundering. Their civilization was the greatest the world has known, but the Sundering broke it. Now they live in scattered enclaves, preserving fragments of what was lost.

## Biology & Physiology
Humanoid but taller and more slender than humans. All Eldari possess mana-sight — the ability to perceive the Weave directly. This makes them natural magic users, though the strength varies. They age slowly and remain physically vital until the last century of life.

## Culture & Society
Governed by councils of elders. Knowledge preservation is the highest cultural value. The Eldari keep the longest written histories and maintain the old Weave Gates.

## History
Dominated the Age of Dawn. The Sundering, caused by Lord Vexis's attempt to seize the Crown of Ages, shattered their civilization. Most of their great cities fell. The survivors retreated to hidden enclaves.
