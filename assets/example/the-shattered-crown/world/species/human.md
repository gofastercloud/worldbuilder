## Overview
Humans are the most numerous and adaptable of the sapient species. They build kingdoms, wage wars, and forget lessons with equal vigor. Their short lifespans drive an urgency that other species find either inspiring or alarming.

## Biology & Physiology
Standard humanoid. Highly variable in appearance across races. No innate magical abilities but capable of learning Weave manipulation through training.

## Culture & Society
Feudal kingdoms are the dominant social structure, though the Steppe Folk maintain nomadic traditions. Humans value legacy — they build monuments, write histories, and establish dynasties precisely because they know their individual time is short.

## History
Rose to dominance during the Age of Crowns after the Sundering shattered the Eldari civilization. Founded Valdris and most of the current major settlements.
