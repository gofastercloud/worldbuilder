## Overview
The Stonewardens are master builders and crafters who dwell in mountain halls. Where the Eldari shape magic, the Stonewardens shape stone and metal with unmatched skill.

## Biology & Physiology
Short but immensely strong. Darkvision and tremorsense from millennia of underground life. Naturally resistant to magic — spells have reduced effect on them, but they also struggle to cast.
