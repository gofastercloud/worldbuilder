## Description
The Silver Order is the primary military and peacekeeping force of Valdris and the surrounding territories. Part knightly order, part city guard, part secret society — they guard the Weave Gates and keep the peace in a world still recovering from the War of Broken Crowns.

## History & Founding
Founded shortly after Valdris itself, by Queen Morwen's decree. Originally tasked with guarding the Weave Gates discovered during the city's construction. Over time, their mandate expanded to general defense and peacekeeping.

## Structure & Hierarchy
Commander (currently Kael Dawnblade, contested) → Captains → Blades → Initiates. Roughly 800 active members. Promotion by merit and trial, not birth.

## Beliefs & Values
Duty before self. The blade shields, it does not conquer. Guard the Gates. Remember the Sundering. These tenets are drilled into every initiate.

## Territory & Influence
Based in the Silver Bastion within Valdris. Patrols extend to all major roads within a week's ride. Limited influence beyond that — they are a Valdrian institution, not a continental one.

## Notable Members
- **Kael Dawnblade** — Current (contested) Commander
- **Aldric Dawnblade** — Former Commander (deceased)
- **Queen Morwen** — Founder (deceased)

## Notes
The Order's real power lies not in numbers but in their guardianship of the Weave Gates. Control the gates, control magical transit.
