## Physical Description
Tall and lean with the Dawnblade golden-brown eyes and strong jaw. Dark hair worn short in military fashion. A fresh scar runs from his left temple to his jawline — a souvenir from the War of Broken Crowns. Moves with a soldier's economy, always aware of exits. Wears the battered Silver Order surcoat over practical leather armor.

## Personality
Brave to the point of recklessness — a Dawnblade trait. Deeply loyal to those he considers his, but haunted by watching Queen Morwen die. Struggles with anger he can't direct. Speaks bluntly, sometimes to his detriment.

## Backstory
Born in Valdris in 2A 215. Son of Aldric Dawnblade, founder of their minor noble house, and Sera, a healer with secret Weave sensitivity. Lost his father in the first year of the War of Broken Crowns. Taken under Queen Morwen's wing — she saw potential in him. Witnessed her murder by Lord Vexis at the war's end.

## Motivations & Goals
Primary: Avenge Queen Morwen. Secondary: Understand his strange ability to occasionally perceive the Weave. Tertiary: Prove worthy of leading House Dawnblade.

## Voice & Mannerisms
Short sentences. Military vocabulary. Drums his fingers when thinking — a nervous habit his father had. Unconsciously reaches for his sword hilt when uncomfortable. Says "steel and sun" as both greeting and goodbye.
